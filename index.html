About Us

We are a company in engineering and consulting for laboratory equipment. MRW Services was established in 2014 and have over 25 years of knowledge and expertise. Our expertise and knowledge of equipment includes; usage, maintenance, calibration, IQ (Installation Qualification), OQ (Operational Qualification) and PQ (Performance Qualification). We also have Regulatory expertise and knowledge with FDA, USDA, EP, ISO and GAMP guidelines.

  

Our consulting services covers calibration/qualification programs, equipment training modules, equipment maintenance, SOP’s, work instructions and gap assessments. We also write and execute PM/Calibration procedures and IOPQ Protocols. Our company is an Authorized Service Provider for Agilent Technologies (HPLCs, GCs, GCHS and UVVIS Spectrophotometers).

 

We ensure that all our customers receive high-quality service with flexible scheduling

LC Preventative Maintenance and Operational Qualification (PMOQ):

  

Performing a PM on an LC prior to the OQ. A PM consists of consumable replacement such as, replacing pump seals, gold seals, frit, needle and needle seat. Cleaning and checking individual modules. After Completion of the PM, an OQ is completed per the Equipment Qualification Plan. The OQ consists of performing several chemical tests to demonstrate the system is in a qualified state.

  

  

  

  

  

  

GC Preventative Maintenance and Operational Qualification (PMOQ):

  

Performing a PM on a GC prior to the OQ. This PM consists of consumable replacement of septums, liners, split vent cartridge, gold seals, and jets (if all or any are applicable). Cleaning and checking the autosampler tray, injection towers and injection needle for any damages. After completion of the PM, an OQ is completed per the Equipment Qualification Plan.
